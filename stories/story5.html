🧙 The Invisible Wizard’s Pants

In the land of Mistywhirl, there lived a wizard named Waldo. He was very wise and very kind. But Waldo had one little problem—his pants kept disappearing.

“Not again!” he would shout every morning as he looked in the mirror. His shirt, shoes, and hat were there. But his pants? Completely invisible.

It all started when he tried to cast a spell to clean his laundry faster. “Sparkle-wash and vanish dirt!” he chanted. But he sneezed during the last word, and instead of washing the pants, he made them vanish forever.

Now everywhere he went, people whispered, “There goes Waldo... the wizard with invisible pants!” It was very embarrassing.

One day, while walking through the village market, a child asked, “Mr. Wizard, where are your pants?” Waldo turned red like a tomato. “They’re here,” he said. “Just... invisible.”

The villagers didn’t laugh. Instead, they began to love his invisible pants. Some said it was a fashion trend. Others said he must be super magical.

Waldo tried to bring his pants back with every spell he knew. “Un-hide! Re-appear! Materialize!” But nothing worked. Even a magical tailor couldn’t help. “Sorry,” the tailor said. “Invisible cloth is very tricky.”

One day, Waldo helped a squirrel find her lost acorns using a tracking charm. In return, she gave him a golden acorn. “Make a wish,” she squeaked.

Waldo closed his eyes and wished for something big. Not just pants—but pants that sparkled, stretched, and never disappeared.

*POOF!* A pair of magical pants appeared on him! They shimmered with stars and had pockets that sang. He jumped with joy. “I’m fully dressed again!”

From that day on, Waldo wore his magical pants with pride. He started a pants-help club for other wizards with wardrobe troubles. They called it “The Trouser Tower.”

And so, Waldo the wizard learned that even small problems—like missing pants—can lead to big laughs and new friendships.
