🪵 The Talking Log Who Ran for Mayor

In the quiet town of Pinebark, the animals lived happily. Rabbits bounced, squirrels sang, and birds flew in circles just for fun. But one thing was missing: a good mayor. The old mayor, Mr. Moose, always fell asleep during meetings!

One morning, near the river, the animals found something strange — a log with eyes! The log blinked, stretched its tiny arms made of twigs, and said, “Good morning! I’m Larry the Log.”

Everyone gasped. “A talking log?” asked a duck. “That’s impossible!”

But Larry smiled and said, “I may be made of wood, but I’m full of ideas.”

Larry helped build a library out of mushroom walls. He stopped a beaver flood using a hat and a spoon. He even taught chipmunks how to plant cookie seeds. (They didn’t grow cookies, but they grew happy!)

One day, the animals shouted, “Larry for Mayor!” Larry laughed. “But I’m just a log!” Still, he agreed to try. He wore a flower crown and held speeches on top of a giant snail.

“I promise no meetings will be boring!” he shouted. “I’ll make the town fun, fair, and full of snacks!”

The birds clapped their wings, and the turtles danced slowly. Even grumpy Mr. Moose smiled a little.

Larry won the election! On his first day as mayor, he declared “Pajama Tuesday” and made sure every tree got a hug.

He built rainbow slides between treehouses and placed tiny mailboxes for ants. Every creature had a voice, even the shy worms.

“You’re the best mayor ever!” said a squirrel.

Larry the Log blushed (as much as wood could) and said, “When you listen with your heart, you don’t need legs to lead.”

And so, Pinebark became the happiest forest in the world — all thanks to one kind, clever, talking log who truly cared.
